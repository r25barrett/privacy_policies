Privacy Policy

MSCVL Fitness Tracker is built on a simple principle: your data is yours. We have no interest in collecting it, storing it, or profiting from it.

1 Who We Are

MSCVL is an independent fitness tracking app for Android. The app is provided as-is with no backend infrastructure, no user accounts, and no cloud sync.

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at: mscvl24@gmail.com

2 Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not operate any servers that receive your personal data. The following information is stored exclusively on your device and never transmitted to us:
  • Workout history, sets, reps, and weights
  • Exercise performance records (1RM, cardio times, etc.)
  • Muscle fatigue and recovery data
  • Gym equipment configuration
  • App preferences and settings
  • Any health data read from Google Health Connect

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, location, IP address, device identifiers (beyond what AdMob requires — see §4), or any other personally identifiable information.

3 Local Storage

The app uses Hive, an on-device embedded database, to store all workout and configuration data. This data:

  • Never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it
  • Is not backed up to any cloud service by MSCVL
  • Can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the app

Android's standard backup system (Google One Backup) may back up app data to your own Google account if you have that feature enabled in your device settings. That backup is governed by Google's own privacy policy, not ours.

4 Advertising (Google AdMob)

This is the one area where a third party does collect data. The app displays ads provided by Google AdMob to help cover development costs.

Google AdMob may collect and use:

  • An advertising identifier (Android Advertising ID / GAID)
  • Device model and OS version
  • IP address (to approximate general location for ad targeting)
  • App usage signals (ad impressions, clicks)

This data is collected and processed by Google, not by us. Google's privacy policy applies: policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time in your Android device settings: Settings → Privacy → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

5 Health Connect

If you grant permission, the app may read data from Google Health Connect (e.g. heart rate, step count). This data is:

  • Read from Health Connect and displayed within the app
  • Never transmitted to our servers (we have none)
  • Never shared with third parties by MSCVL

You can revoke Health Connect permissions at any time in Settings → Apps → Health Connect → App permissions.

6 Your Data Export

You can export your complete workout history at any time, for free. We believe you should always own and control the data you create — no paid subscription required, no hoops to jump through.

The export feature is available in the app's History tab. Exported files are saved to your device storage or shared to any app you choose. MSCVL never sees or receives those files.

7 Children's Privacy

MSCVL is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through this app, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

8 Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy in a meaningful way (for example, if we add a new third-party SDK that collects data), we will update the effective date at the top of this page and note the change in the app's release notes.

Because we collect no personal data ourselves, most typical privacy policy changes will not affect you.
