1 Who We Are

1500 Scorecard is an independent scorecard app for the card game 1500, developed by r25barrett. The app is provided as-is for personal, non-commercial use.

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at: mscvl24@gmail.com

2 Account Types

The app supports two ways to play:

  • Guest (anonymous): No email, no Google account required. Firebase assigns you an anonymous ID. Your data is stored locally on your device and is lost if you uninstall or sign out without first linking to Google.
  • Google Sign-In: Sign in with your Google account to back up your stats to the cloud and access them across devices. Your email address is used by Firebase Authentication to identify your account; it is not stored in our database or used for marketing.
You are never required to create an account. All core game features work with a guest session.

3 Data We Collect

When you use a Google account, the following data is stored in Firebase Realtime Database (operated by Google) under your account:

  • Display name — the name you choose in the app
  • Game statistics — games played, games won, high scores, finishing positions
  • Per-game records — date, player count, your final score, win/loss, rounds played

We do not collect your email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, device identifiers, or any other personal information beyond what is listed above.

Multiplayer game records are shared among all players in that game. Your display name may appear in records visible to other participants.

4 Local Storage

The app also stores data locally on your device using SharedPreferences:

  • Your unique app identifier (auto-generated, not linked to your identity)
  • Local game history and player records
  • App settings and colour preferences

This local data never leaves your device unless you are signed in with Google, in which case completed games are synced to Firebase as described in §3.

5 Firebase & Google Sign-In

Firebase is operated by Google. When you sign in with Google or sync data to the cloud, Google's infrastructure processes that data.

Firebase Authentication stores your Google account credentials (email address and account ID) on Google's servers in order to authenticate you. This is governed by Google's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

We do not sell, share, or otherwise disclose any data to third parties beyond the Firebase/Google infrastructure that powers the app.

6 Advertising (Google AdMob)

The app displays ads provided by Google AdMob to help cover development costs. Google AdMob may collect and use:

  • An advertising identifier (Android Advertising ID / GAID)
  • Device model and OS version
  • IP address (to approximate general location for ad targeting)
  • App usage signals (ad impressions, clicks)

This data is collected and processed by Google, not by us. Google's privacy policy applies: policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalised advertising at any time in your Android device settings: Settings → Privacy → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation.

7 Deleting Your Data

You can permanently delete your account and all associated cloud data at any time, directly from within the app:

  • Open the app and tap the Account icon
  • Scroll to the Danger Zone section
  • Tap Delete Account and confirm

This immediately and permanently deletes your profile, all game statistics, and your Firebase Authentication account. Local device data is also cleared. This action cannot be undone.

You may also submit a deletion request by email at mscvl24@gmail.com. Manual requests are processed within 30 days. Full instructions are available at: Delete Account page.

No retention period. Account data is deleted immediately upon confirmation. Nothing is kept.

8 Children's Privacy

1500 Scorecard is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through this app, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to remove that data.
